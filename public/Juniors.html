MK CYCLONES DODGEBALL

Juniors

We are a friendly and inclusive club that welcomes all young people aged 8-16 to come and try dodgeball. Our weekly sessions are a great way to improve your skills, meet new friends and have fun.

Our juniors sessions take place every Wednesday from 7-8pm at Courtside Sports Hall in Bletchley (MK3 6DR). The cost is just £3 per session, but the first session is FREE!.

What to expect at our sessions

Our experienced coaches will guide you through a range of dodgeball drills, games and challenges that will help you to develop your skills and confidence. We focus on creating a positive and supportive environment where everyone can thrive.

How to join

If you're interested in joining our juniors club, simply come along to one of our sessions and give it a try. We recommend wearing comfortable sports clothing and indoor trainers.

Our training facility at Courtside Sports Hall is second-to-none, with plenty of space, including a viewing area for parents to stay and watch.

If you have any questions about our juniors club, please get in touch using the contact page, and make sure to sign up using the google form below.
